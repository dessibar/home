Institute of Marine Research (MFRI)

 

MFRI was established 1 July 2016, by merging the Marine Research Institute (MRI, established in 1965) and the Institute of Freshwater Fisheries (established in 1946).

FMRI is a government institute under the auspices of the Ministry of Industry and Innovation. MFRI provides the Ministry with scientific advice based on its research on marine resources and the environment.
